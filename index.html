Some Interesting Facts About Matcha
Matcha was first discovered by Japanese farmers who accidentally created it during a harsh winter
Matcha is known to increase brain power and helps you focus more
Matcha is a versatile ingredient that can be used to make not only drinks, but also food
A matcha's grade can be identified by its aroma! A more buttery aroma means a higher matcha grade
The very act of preparing and serving tea encourages conversation. The little spaces in time created by teatime rituals call out to be filled with conversation. Even the tea itself–warm and comforting–inspires a feeling of relaxation and trust that fosters shared confidences.
- Emilie Barnes
